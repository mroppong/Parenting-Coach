Liam's Profile

Key Temperament Traits

How to Best Communicate

  • Use a calm, steady voice, especially when he is feeling intense emotions.
  • Acknowledge his feelings before offering solutions. "I see you're feeling frustrated right now."
  • Give him choices to foster his sense of autonomy. "Would you like to clean up now or in 5 minutes?"

How to Support Him When He's Upset

  • His high reactivity means he needs space first. Let him withdraw to a safe corner before approaching.
  • Offer physical comfort like a hug or a back rub once he's ready to accept it.
  • Help him label his big feelings. "It sounds like you're feeling disappointed that we have to leave."

Core Needs

  • Predictability: Consistent routines help him feel secure and manage his high energy levels.
  • Autonomy: He thrives when he has a sense of control over his activities and choices.
  • Connection: Quality one-on-one time helps him regulate and feel understood.
